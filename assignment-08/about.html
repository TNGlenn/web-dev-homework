Why I Chose Painting

I chose to teach painting a self portrait because it's something I've been working on for a few weeks and I have plenty of in progress shots.
It's also something I'm passionate about so I knew I wouldn't get tired of looking at these images while crafting this site.
I think the best way to learn more about painting is to follow along a tutorial on Youtube or take a class on a platform like Skillshare.
In person classes and critiques can be really helpful but online resources can be less intimidating to beginners.
