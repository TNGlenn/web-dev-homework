HOW TO PAINT A SELF-PORTRAIT IN OILS


First take a reference image. Try to have some interesting lighting going on so you have a lot of lights and darks to explore.

If your canvas or surface isn't sealed, seal it with a layer of gesso, sand this surface down, then gesso a second layer.

Lay in some lights and darks and use a paint thinner like Gamsol to add sketch in basic features.

Measure proportions with your brush to figure.

Go in and add some detail to different areas.

Start working in some color.

Work the color in and work with the tone while correcting some areas.

Make sure you're working with your reference!

Stand back from your painting occasionally to check that everything is proportional.

Mess up the left eye really badly.

Do some last minute tweaks but don't fix the eye, that's too hard.

Post details of the parts you're actually proud of for miniscule clout on Instagram.
